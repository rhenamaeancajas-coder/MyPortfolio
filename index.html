About Me

Rhena Mae Ancajas is a Licensed Professional Teacher and a proud graduate of the Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEED), dedicated to nurturing young learners through creativity, patience, and innovative teaching methods. With a passion for shaping minds and fostering a love for learning, she designs engaging lessons that not only meet academic goals but also inspire curiosity and confidence in her students. Her commitment to education goes beyond the classroom, as she continuously seeks opportunities to grow professionally and contribute positively to her community.
